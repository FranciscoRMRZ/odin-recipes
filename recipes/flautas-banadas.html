Flautas bañadas

Description

We could define flautas bañadas as a rolled deep-fried chicken taco. They are one of many staples of mexican gastronomy and could be used as an entree for other dishes.

Its ingredients could be found in any mexican pantry and are a good option to learn mexican couisine.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium sized potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 small piece of queso fresco
  • Oregano
  • 7 tomatoes
  • A handful of chile de árbol
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Half a cabbage
  • 1 carrot
  • A handful of cilantro
  • Enchilada tortillas
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Oil
  • White vinegar

Steps

  1. Boil together with enough water to cover it all the chiles de árbol, garlic, 1/4 of onion and tomatoes to make the salsa.
  2. Fine chop the cabbage and clean it.
  3. Fine chop half an onion and the cilantro
  4. Peel and fine grate the carrot
  5. Mix the cabbage, onion, cilantro and carrot. Add white vinegar, salt, and oregano to taste.
  6. Boil, let cool and peel the potatoes.
  7. In a hot pan, add 1/4 onion, salt to taste and the boiled potatoes
  8. Mash the potatoes and add half the queso fresco
  9. Mix thoroughly the queso with the potatoes.
  10. Let the potato mix simmer for 5 minutes
  11. Put a little of potato mix in a flauta tortilla and roll tightly
  12. Put enough oil in a frying pan to cover the flautitas, and bring it to frying tmeperature.
  13. Deep-fry the flautitas in oil, and place on a separate plate.
  14. Serve the flautitas and cover with the cabbage salad. Add chile sauce to taste
  15. Enjoy.
