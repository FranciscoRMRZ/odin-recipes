Arroz blanco

Description

Arroz blanco is the fist kind of rice we learn to cook before making more complicated recipes. It is a good base for a complete meal and, with enough experience, it take no time to get it ready for dinner.

On top of that, we can combine any kind of topping with it and they will complement each other

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long-grain rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley (optional)
